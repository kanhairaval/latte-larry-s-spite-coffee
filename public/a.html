Scary Affogato

An Affogato is an Italian coffee-based dessert that consists of a shot of hot espresso poured over a scoop of vanilla ice cream. The combination of hot and cold creates a rich, creamy, and delicious treat that is typically served in a tall glass. Some variations of the Affogato may also include a drizzle of chocolate sauce or a sprinkling of cocoa powder to enhance the flavor.
